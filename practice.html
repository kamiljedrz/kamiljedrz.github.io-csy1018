Practice

Models of Good Practice (MGP) in User Interface (UI) Design

For my website design I have used many websites as an inspiration before I have created my own website. This is because the websites evolve so it is important that you use features that already exist on the websites that are updated frequently. By using other websites as an example, I was able to enchance the look and functionality of my own website. I was also able to use best practices that made my website stands out in the way that it is very easy to read, understand, and most importantly, it is very basic to use and retrieve any information’s I have provided.

I’ve been worked with Adrian Sompolinski and Erhan Koc. We have shared our design ideas by looking at the most popular websites to pick up the features and visual design that stands out and the one’s that are frequently used on the sites to make sure that we are meeting the website standards.

I have located my company logo on the top left corner of the website, and a hamburger menu on the right side of the website in the mobile version. This has allowed me to keep my top bar neat and easy to understand by the user. To design this, I have been looking on the website called TypingClub.

On the homepage I have created rounded profile pictures as they look very neat and they present way better than the square ones. For this idea, I have used the website called SpringCm.

For my content section I have made the 80% width website, so it is not a full screen. I have done this as I like to include the background in the design as this makes the website more eye catchy. For this, I was looking at one of the most popular social media website called Instagram.

I was looking for something simple for my footer as I have included everything in the header and the content, so I wanted the footer to include only few links, as well as All rights reserved text that is essential on the website. To do my footer, I was looking at the website called Wikipedia.

This is a list of the inspirational websites I have looked at when creating my own design.

Instagram - Website Width
